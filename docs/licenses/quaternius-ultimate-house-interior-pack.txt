The FBX source pack is retained under pre-assets/2nd pack. Door_3,
Window_Large2 and Window_Small2 are converted to standalone GLB files and used
by the production build for the doors and windows of every current room.
Couch_L, Fireplace, Shelf_Large, Curtains_Double, Carpet_Round and
Light_Chandelier are also converted to standalone GLB files for the Living
Room. No additional external source is used for that room.
